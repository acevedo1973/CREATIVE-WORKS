About Me

S. Angelo Acevedo is a dynamic singer and actor who has graced stages across Northern California, bringing characters to life with depth and authenticity. From playing the loveable "Dr. Bird" in Cedeya Marley's "Three Lil Birds" to leading the band Electric Arrows in Bay Area clubs, including the historic Fillmore's legendary Boom Boom Room. He has featured in Rockefeller.org documentaries, and national cable TV on the Investigation Discovery Channel, his talent speaks for itself.

Most recently, he has performed in works by playwright Reginald Clay, embracing stories that keep artistry fresh and relevant. Shaye continues to find a home in his artistic expression and craft.
