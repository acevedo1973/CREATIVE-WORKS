About S. Angelo Acevedo

S. Angelo Acevedo is a dynamic singer and actor who has captivated audiences across Northern California, bringing characters to life with depth and authenticity. From playing the loveable Dr. Bird in Cedeya Marley's *Three Little Birds* to leading the band Electric Arrows in Bay Area clubs—including the legendary Boom Boom Room at The Fillmore—his performances exude energy and passion.

His talent extends beyond the stage, with featured appearances in Rockefeller.org documentaries and national cable television, including the Investigation Discovery Channel. Most recently, he has embraced powerful storytelling through the works of playwright Reginald Clay, ensuring that artistry remains fresh, relevant, and deeply resonant.
