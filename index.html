About Me

S. Angelo Acevedo is a dynamic singer and actor who has captivated audiences across Northern California, bringing characters to life with depth and authenticity. From playing the loveable Dr. Bird in Cedeya Marley's Three Little Birds to fronting the Electric Arrows in Bay Area clubs—including the historic Boom Boom Room at The Fillmore—his performances exude energy and passion. Beyond the stage, his talent has been featured in Rockefeller.org documentaries and on national cable television, including appearances on the Investigation Discovery Channel. Most recently, he has embraced powerful storytelling through the works of playwright Reginald Clay, ensuring that artistry remains fresh, relevant, and deeply resonant. With a grand and elegant presence, Shaye continues to make his mark, finding home in his artistic expression and craft.
